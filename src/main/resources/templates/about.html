Easy and quick service to
learn information about

Computer components service was established in 2020, in city Vinnitsa, Ukraine. Our web-application allows you to know more about computer structure and its elements. In sections of this app you can check actual information about computer components, compare components by their price and characteristics and build web version of your personal computer.

It will help everyone comfortably and effortlessly assess the situation in the market. On this Github page u can see all changes throughout the life of the project.
