Information about app

Hello, my name is Dima. I'm developer of this web-application. This app designed to give people knowledge about computer and its components : what is important to buy and what is not. In sections of my app you can check actual information about computer components, compare components by their price and characteristics and build web version of your personal computer. It will help everyone comfortably and effortlessly assess the situation in the market. On this Github page u can see all changes throughout the life of the project. My web-app allows you to help to increase count of computer components on my site using "Component constructor". When u construct new component it will be sent to my e-mail where I check the correctness of this data. In a positive case this component will be added to general list. So be active and this app will develop faster)
